## STEP 5: ADD AUDIO (10 minutes)

2. Import your voiceover file
3. Drag to Audio Track 1

### Add Music
1. CapCut Library ‚Üí Search "sports" or "electronic"
2. Find upbeat track (120-140 BPM)
3. Drag to Audio Track 2
4. Volume: -20dB (background level)

### Add Sound Effects
1. CapCut Library ‚Üí Search:
   - "whoosh" (transitions)
   - "notification" (alerts)
   - "success" (wins)
   - "pop" (buttons)
2. Add at key moments

### Mix Audio
- Voiceover: 0dB (full volume)
- Music: -20dB (background)
- Sound FX: -10dB
